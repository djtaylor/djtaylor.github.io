About Me

I’m a software engineer who’s been in the IT industry since 2008. My career path has taken me from front end web design, to database administration, and systems programming. I have a passion for Python, well designed UI, graphic design, and teaching people about the possibilities of programming.

I started in Washington, D.C., moved to Philadelphia for a year long stint as a database administrator, then went traveling. I ended up in Bangkok working as a systems and web programmer. My experiences and travels inspire my code.
